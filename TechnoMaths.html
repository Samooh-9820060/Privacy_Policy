Information Collection and Use

While TechnoMaths does not collect any personally identifiable information, we do gather certain types of data to improve app functionality and user experience:

  • Device Information: We collect specific device details such as brand, model, and product for Android devices, and identifierForVendor for iOS devices.
  • Game Scores and Usernames: These details are stored to maintain leaderboards and provide a competitive environment for our users.
  • Notification Click Counts: We track the number of times users click on our notifications, but not on the advertisements.
  • Ad Preferences: We store your preferences regarding personalized ads. This preference is used to determine the type of ads shown to you.

Data collected is encrypted in transit, and securely stored using Firebase Firestore.

The app may use third-party services, such as Google AdMob, to display advertisements. Depending on your preferences and consent, these services may collect information, such as your device's advertising identifier, to serve personalized ads. You can find more details on how Google uses information from sites or apps that utilize their services at https://www.google.com/policies/technologies/partner-sites/.
