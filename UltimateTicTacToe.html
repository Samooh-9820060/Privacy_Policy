Information Collection and Use

Ultimate Tic Tac Toe does not collect any personally identifiable information. The game uses Google Play sign-in and Firebase Firestore to enable online play functionality:

  • Firebase Firestore: We use Firestore to store online game data, enabling users to play against each other. We do not store any personal or sensitive user data in Firestore.

Our app does not currently feature any ads, nor does it use any analytics services to collect data on user behavior within the app.
